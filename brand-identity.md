# Uplift Dental Brand Identity — Extracted from Logos

## Logo Design
- **Wordmark style**: Large decorative serif/display typeface for "uplift" — looks like a custom or high-end serif with elegant curves (similar to Cormorant Garamond or a custom serif)
- **"up"** in lowercase — slightly lighter/smaller than "lift"
- **"lift"** in lowercase — taller, more prominent, with the "l" and "t" having elegant serifs
- **"dental & orthodontics"** — small, light italic or regular weight text below/beside the wordmark
- **Tooth icon** — a soft, rounded tooth silhouette in light gray/off-white, positioned behind/beside the text as a watermark element
- **"U" monogram** — standalone icon version: decorative script "U" overlaid on the tooth silhouette

## Brand Colors
- **Primary Teal (Dark)**: Deep teal-green — approximately #1A6B6B or #0D6E6E (rich, saturated teal)
  - Logo-01 shows the darkest, most saturated version
  - OKLCH approximate: oklch(0.42 0.09 195)
- **Primary Teal (Light/Muted)**: A lighter, more muted teal-blue — approximately #7BAAB5 or #6B9FA8
  - Used in Logo-02 (the "up" portion is very light/muted, "lift" is mid teal)
  - OKLCH approximate: oklch(0.65 0.06 210)
- **Tooth Gray**: Very light warm gray — approximately #EBEBEB or #E8E8E8
  - The tooth silhouette background element
- **Kids Green accent**: Bright lime/apple green — approximately #7DC242 or #85C441
  - Used in Logo-09 and Logo-10 for "Kids" text
  - OKLCH approximate: oklch(0.72 0.15 135)
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)

## Typography in Logo
- Main wordmark: Elegant serif with decorative swash terminals — reminiscent of Cormorant Garamond, IM Fell English, or a custom serif
- Subtitle "dental & orthodontics": Light weight, small, elegant — possibly the same serif family in regular/light weight
- "Kids" text: Rounded, playful script

## Logo Variants Available
- Logo-01: Full dark teal wordmark on white (PRIMARY)
- Logo-02: Two-tone — "up" in light muted teal, "lift" in medium teal
- Logo-05: Standalone "U" monogram with tooth silhouette (icon only)
- Logo-09/10: "Kids" variant with green accent
- Other variants likely include: reversed (white on dark), horizontal, stacked, etc.

## Design Direction for Website
- Use Logo-01 (full dark teal) as primary logo on white/light backgrounds
- Use white version on dark teal backgrounds
- Primary color: Deep teal #1A6B6B (oklch ~0.42 0.09 195)
- Secondary/accent: Lighter teal (oklch ~0.65 0.06 210)  
- Background: Clean white with very light teal tints
- Typography: Match the elegant serif feel — use Cormorant Garamond or similar for headings
- The tooth silhouette is a key brand element — use it as decorative motif
