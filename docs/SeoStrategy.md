## Backlink Acquisition Strategy

### Tier 1: Local Citations (Do These First — Free)
These are the most impactful for local SEO:

1. **Google Business Profile** — Claim/verify at business.google.com. Add all services, photos, hours. This is #1 for local rankings.
2. **Yelp for Business** — yelp.com/biz — Respond to all reviews.
3. **Healthgrades** — healthgrades.com — Claim your profile, add all doctors.
4. **Zocdoc** — zocdoc.com — Add all three doctors with their specialties.
5. **WebMD Health Listings** — webmd.com/dentists
6. **Vitals.com** — vitals.com — Claim doctor profiles.
7. **RateMDs** — ratemds.com — Claim profiles.
8. **US News Health** — health.usnews.com/doctors
9. **Bing Places** — bingplaces.com — Mirror your Google Business Profile.
10. **Apple Maps Connect** — mapsconnect.apple.com

### Tier 2: Dental-Specific Directories
11. **1-800-Dentist** — 1800dentist.com
12. **Dentist.com** — dentist.com
13. **Find-a-Dentist (ADA)** — ada.org/find-a-dentist
14. **Angie's List** — angieslist.com
15. **Thumbtack** — thumbtack.com

### Tier 3: Local Orange County Links
16. **Garden Grove Chamber of Commerce** — ggchamber.com — Join and get a member listing link.
17. **Orange County Business Journal** — ocbj.com — Submit a press release about community screenings.
18. **Nextdoor** — nextdoor.com — Create a business page for Garden Grove.

20. **Local news sites** — Contact OC Register, Voice of OC with your community story.

### Tier 4: Content-Based Link Building

## Google Business Profile Optimization

1. **Category**: Primary = "Dentist", Secondary = "Orthodontist", "Oral Surgeon", "Cosmetic Dentist"
2. **Services**: List every service with descriptions and prices where possible
3. **Photos**: Upload 20+ photos — office exterior, interior, team, before/after smiles
4. **Posts**: Post weekly updates — promotions, blog articles, patient spotlights
5. **Q&A**: Pre-populate with your FAQ answers
6. **Reviews**: Ask every satisfied patient to leave a Google review (text them a direct link)
7. **Attributes**: Mark "Wheelchair accessible", "Accepts new patients", "Online appointments"

## Review Generation Strategy

Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor after proximity. Current goal: 200+ reviews.

**How to get more reviews:**
1. Text patients a review link immediately after their appointment
2. Add a QR code in the office linking to your Google review page
3. Train staff to verbally ask: "Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review?"
4. Respond to every review — positive and negative — professionally

**Review link format:**
`https://g.page/r/[YOUR_PLACE_ID]/review`
